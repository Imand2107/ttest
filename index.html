The Personal Fitness Trainer app is designed to help people achieve their fitness goals in a simple and effective way. It starts with BMI calculation, where users can enter their height and weight to understand their current health status. The app also lets users set personal goals, such as losing weight, building muscle, or improving fitness, by breaking them into small, manageable steps. To help users stay on track, it provides easy-to-follow workout plans, beginner-friendly exercises, and video guides to ensure proper form. Users can track their progress over time and adjust their routines as needed. With its simple design, helpful reminders, and flexible features, the app makes it easier for anyone, regardless of fitness level, to stay active and healthy.
